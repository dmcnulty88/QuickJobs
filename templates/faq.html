Frequently Asked Questions

For Job Seekers

What is Quickjobs Portal?

Quickjobs Portal is a web-based platform designed to connect temporary job seekers with job opportunities.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "select User", followed by "Create Account" link on the webpage and follow the instructions.

How do I apply for a job listing?

To apply for a job, job seeker need to create an account and then click on the "Apply" button in their dashboard.

For Employers

How do I post a job listing?

To post a job listing, employers need to create an account and then click on the "Create a new a Job" button in their dashboard.

How can I manage job applications?

You can manage job applications by logging into your employer account and accessing the "View Applicants" on the job listing in your dashboard.
